Olympiads in Nepal
The Road to OLYMPICS of Academics

Introduction

Olympiads are the International competitions held between many countries and hosted by a particular country each season. Each participating nation sends its best team of 5 or 6 to represent themselves in the event.

Following is the list of some International Olympiads:

  • International Science Olympiad (ISO)
  • International Mathematics Olympiad (IMO)
  • International Olympiad of Informatics (IOI)
  • International Olympiad on Astronomy and Astrophysics (IOAA)
  • International Economics Olympiad (IEO)

Olympiads are open to students that have been enrolled in Grade 1 upto those who have not started their University Education.

Nepal in Olympiads

Nepal has not been able to secure a gold medal till now. The scenario of Nepal in Olympiads is not new however, its achievements in these competitions have also not been high.

Organizations for Olympiads in Nepal

Few organizations have taken the lead to conduct the national phase of Olympiads for selection of delegates in the International Olympiad Competitons. Organizations like Mathematical Association of Nepal, Nepal Physics Society and STEM Foundation Nepal have been actively participating in conducting competitions for selecting the national delegates, every year.
